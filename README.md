# FOIPutrajaya2025
Date: 4 - 9 Nov 2025

Venue: Putrajaya International Convention Centre (PICC)
## Background
This festival is a big event to share new ideas and make learning easy for everyone. Its goal is to make Malaysia a center for smart discussions and new inventions. It brings together leaders, experts, and the public to talk about solutions for problems in the country and the world. The festival hopes to create a society that loves knowledge and big ideas. It's also a place to celebrate Malaysia's successes and show off Putrajaya as a "City of Ideas."
